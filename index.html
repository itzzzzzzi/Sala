Sala is an art collection, an archive of culture. It offers a space without physical and institutional boundaries, and redefines the concept of permanence; it seeks to sustainably preserve and showcase contemporary works.

All images undergo a process of sustainable translation: reduced in size, optimized, and uploaded to a server powered by renewable energy (Netlify). The project has a minimetric (small scale, meaningful) environmental impact without compromising the work. The collection is composed of research material by Itziar Soto Sánchez and curated submissions.
